Lebanon's Blog of The Year (2014 edition)

How do we define the "Blog of The Year"? The Blog of the Year in our book is the blog whose posts have the highest probability of becoming #1 on our website. While a post gathers virality across its lifetime, it only has 12 hours to gather enough shares and clicks to get ahead of the pack and become #1 on Lebanese Blogs

Our Winner this year is Ink on The Side, by Sareen Akarjalian, whose posts have an amazing 79.17% chance of becoming #1, making it our Lebanon Blog of the year 2014

An honorary mention also goes to Blog Baladi, who got the highest absolute number of #1 posts in 2014

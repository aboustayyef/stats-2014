Lebanon's Most Viral Blogger

This statistic relies on a measure in Lebanese Blogs we call "Virality". It's a score for each post that combines twitter shares and facebook shares and suppresses extreme variations.

After calculating the average virality for each blogger, we have a winner: Blog of The Boss by Ralph Aoun has the highest average virality score of 36.28 over 50, making him Lebanese Blog's Most Viral Blog of 2014. Below is a table of the top ten most viral blogs this year

Lebanon's Most Viral Blog (2015 edition)

This statistic relies on a measure in Lebanese Blogs we call "Virality". It is a score for each post that combines its Twitter shares and Facebook shares while normalizing extreme variations. (Read more about virality here)

This year's most viral blog is A Separate State of Mind by Elie Fares. It has the highest average* virality score of 47 over 50, making him Lebanon's Most Viral Blog of 2015. Below is a table of the top ten most viral blogs this year

# Blog Average Virality (Maximum: 50)
1 A Separate State of Mind 47
2 Blog Of The Boss 39.5
3 Blog Baladi 35.9
4 Gino's Blog 35.4
5 My Beirut Chronicles 34.7
6 Beirut Report 33.2
7 Marmite et Ponpon 29.1
8 StepFeed Lebanon 27.8
9 Let's Tlk Abt Movies 27.1
10 Ivy Says 24.5

*The virality is calculated for posts that were posted between January 1st 2015 and December 15 2015. Only blogs that published more than 52 posts (average of 1 post per week) in that timeframe were qualified

Lebanon's Most Viral Blogger (2014 edition)

This statistic relies on a measure in Lebanese Blogs we call "Virality". It is a score for each post that combines its Twitter shares and Facebook shares while normalizing extreme variations. (Read more about virality here)

After calculating the average virality for each blogger*, we got a winner: Karl Remarks by Karl Sharro has the highest average virality score of 42.87 over 50, making him Lebanon's Most Viral Blog of 2014. Below is a table of the top twenty most viral blogs this year
